Riff Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026

Riff is a music-sharing app for sending songs, messages, and invites to friends. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We collect account details you provide, including phone number, username, first name, last name, profile preferences, music service preference, and app theme choice. We also store songs you send or save, messages you write, reactions, friends, invite-code activity, blocked users, device notification tokens, and basic diagnostics.

Contacts

If you grant Contacts permission, Riff uses contacts on your device to help you find or invite people you know. Contacts are used for matching and invite flows. We do not sell contacts.

Music And Microphone

If you use song recognition, Riff may briefly access your microphone to identify music playing around you. Riff does not use the microphone for general recording. If you connect Apple Music or Spotify flows, we use those services to search, resolve, and open music links.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate the app, deliver song shares and messages, manage invite codes, send notifications, prevent abuse, enforce limits, troubleshoot outages, and improve reliability.

Service Providers

Riff uses Firebase/Google Cloud for authentication, database, storage, functions, messaging, crash reporting, and logging. Riff may also use Apple, Spotify, Songlink/Odesli, and ChottuLink for music and link features.

Security

We use Firebase Authentication, Firestore security rules, private profile storage, App Check rollout, rate limits, and server-side functions to protect app data. No system is perfectly secure, but we design access around the minimum permissions needed.

Deletion

You can request account deletion in the app. Deletion removes your account profile and private metadata and cleans up related app records where practical. Some historical records may be anonymized or retained when needed for safety, abuse prevention, or legal obligations.

Children

Riff is not intended for children under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Riff team through the support channel listed in the app or App Store listing.
